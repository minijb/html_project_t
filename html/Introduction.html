Summary

The Ocean Sediment Database (OSD) is a platform for collecting sedimentary data from the international oceanic drilling program, including core sedimentological data such as material composition (macroscopic, microscopic), sedimentary geochemistry (organic, inorganic, interstitial water) and physical properties and is planned to be linked to the oceanic sediment age model. The temporal-spatial-material data organization method correlates sediment data to solve key scientific problems such as the evolution of oceanic sedimentary material, and provides cases-driven support for the construction of oceanic sediment knowledge graph.
